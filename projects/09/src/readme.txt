The compiled VM files are in the vm folder, the Jack source files, source folder.

This is a simple slash them all game. When you run the VM files, there will be a prompt and some instructions. You can press Enter to start the game.

To win the game, you need to kill 30 enemies. When the game starts, a man that you control will be created at the center of the screen. You can press arrow keys to change the direction that he faces, and Z key to slash his sword. The slash spans 2 units of space. A total of 30 enemies will randomly spawn from 4 directions, gradually moving towards you. You need to slash them before they touch you. They will move faster as the game goes by. The game is over when you get touched. You can quit the game by pressing Q.
